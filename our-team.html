Oscar Onyango

Project Director

Oscar Onyango is an impact-driven engineer and technology leader with a background in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (BEng) and Information System Security (MSc). He brings over five years of experience in project management, IoT, renewable energy, and organizational development. As Project Director at Decapol Solutions Limited, Oscar provides strategic leadership in engineering design, technical innovation, and community-focused infrastructure development. His career spans research, product development, and capacity building across organizations like @iLabAfrica, AHAINNOVATE, and Spearhead Technologies. Passionate about sustainability and digital transformation, he champions the use of technology to improve lives and foster resilient communities across Kenya.
